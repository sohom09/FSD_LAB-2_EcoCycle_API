🔍 How the Video API Works Here

The HTMLVideoElement inherits from HTMLMediaElement. Key properties: video.play(), video.pause(), video.currentTime, video.duration, video.volume, video.playbackRate, video.muted. We also use video.requestFullscreen() for fullscreen mode and video.requestPictureInPicture() for PiP. The timeupdate event syncs our custom seek bar in real-time. We use a <canvas> to capture frames with ctx.drawImage(video, ...).
