Understanding E-Waste Recycling

Electronic waste includes discarded electronic devices such as mobile phones, batteries, chargers, laptops and computers.

Proper recycling helps recover valuable materials while reducing environmental pollution and supporting sustainability initiatives.

Benefits of E-Waste Recycling

  • Reduces pollution
  • Conserves natural resources
  • Promotes sustainability
  • Encourages responsible disposal
